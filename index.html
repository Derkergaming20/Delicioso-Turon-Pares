About Us

Our business, "Delicioso Turon Pares", is a student-run kamote-saging

turon shop partnered with an ice candy stand. We aim to provide

delicious and affordable kamote turon and refreshing ice candy

to customers in the Philippines. Our goal is to establish

Delicioso Turon Pares as a popular snack to eat,

offering a unique combination of kamote-saging turon

and ice candy to cater to the preferences of students
